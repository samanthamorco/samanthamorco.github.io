About Me

My name is Samantha Morco, but I go by Sami. I'm born and raised in the San Francisco Bay Area, but I relocated to New York in January 2017. I'm a full-stack web developer whose goal in life is to help people by creating amazing technology that will enhance the daily lives of everyone who uses it.

I attended UC Berkeley where I graduated with a bachelor's in integrative biology. During my time at Berkeley, I took a computer science course for non-majors where I learned Snap! and took a student-taught web design course and fell in love with coding. I decided to enroll in the immersive coding bootcamp Actualize and I've been involved with tech ever since.
